Catherine Bruce

PhD Candidate in Fractal Geometry

About Me

I am final year PhD student at The University of Manchester, under the supervision of Dr Xiong Jin. I am interested in ergodic theory, dynamical systems, fractal geometry and probability theory.

Research

My general research interests are in the fields of ergodic theory and dynamical systems, fractal geometry and probability thoery. Recently, my work has largely revolved around strong Marstrand projection theorems for dynamically defined fractal measures.
